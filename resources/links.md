## Claude Model Family

| Model | Best For | Speed |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Claude Haiku** | Quick tasks, simple questions, high-volume processing | Fastest |
| **Claude Sonnet** | Balanced tasks, everyday use, good quality + speed | Balanced |
| **Claude Opus** | Complex analysis, nuanced writing, difficult reasoning | Most capable |
