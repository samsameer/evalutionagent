**Claude Cowork Mastery — March 2026**
**Instructor:** Jabbir Basha, Principal AI Engineer

---

## Module Overview

This is one of the most important modules in the entire course. Here, we dive into the deeper technical understanding of how Claude works — specifically, the **Skills system**, **Agent Roles**, and the broader **Anthropic ecosystem** that powers everything behind the scenes.

By the end of this module, you will move from being a casual user of Claude Cowork to someone who truly understands the engine under the hood and can configure it to work exactly the way you need.

---

## Learning Objectives

By completing this module, you will be able to:

1. **Understand the Claude Skills system** — what skills are, how they work, and how to use them effectively in your daily workflows.
2. **Create and configure Agent Roles** — design specialized personas that shape how Claude approaches different types of tasks, improving quality and consistency.
3. **Navigate the Anthropic ecosystem** — understand the full suite of Anthropic products, models, and resources, and how Claude Cowork fits into the bigger picture.
