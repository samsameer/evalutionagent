## What is Claude Cowork?

Claude Cowork is Anthropic's breakthrough **AI agent** that goes far beyond traditional chatbots. While a chatbot responds to questions with text, Cowork **takes action** on your behalf:

- **Reads and writes files** on your computer
- **Creates and edits spreadsheets** with real data
- **Browses the web** to research markets, competitors, and opportunities
- **Organizes your folders** automatically
- **Processes documents** like PDFs, receipts, and invoices
- **Runs recurring tasks** on autopilot

Think of it as hiring a highly capable virtual assistant who lives on your computer and can handle the administrative tasks that consume your day.

| Traditional AI Chatbot | Claude Cowork Agent |
