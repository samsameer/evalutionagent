# Module 04 | Claude Cowork Mastery — March 2026

## Data Extraction & Reporting

**Instructor:** Jabbir Basha, Principal AI Engineer

---

### Overview

Data lives everywhere — in PDFs, scanned receipts, invoices, and financial statements. Extracting that data manually is tedious and error-prone. In this module, you will learn how to use Claude Cowork to pull structured information out of unstructured documents and turn it into clean, actionable reports.

By the end of this module, you will be able to hand Cowork a stack of messy PDFs and walk away with a polished spreadsheet or financial summary.

---

### Learning Objectives

- **Extract data from PDFs** — Convert tables, text, and figures locked inside PDF files into editable spreadsheet formats.
- **Process receipts and invoices** — Automate the tedious work of reading receipts, pulling out key fields, and organizing everything into categorized records.
- **Generate financial reports** — Turn raw extracted data into meaningful expense summaries, budget trackers, and period-over-period comparisons.
