## About This Course

Are you overwhelmed by manual data entry, scattered files, and the endless hours required for market research?

**Claude Cowork is a game-changer** for business owners, freelancers, and project managers. Unlike standard AI chatbots, Cowork is an **"agent"**. It doesn't just talk — **it does**. It can access your local folders, read your documents, generate and edit spreadsheets, and even browse the web to complete multi-step tasks while you focus on high-level strategy.

This course is designed specifically for **non-techies and beginners**. We strip away the jargon and show you exactly how to set up Claude Cowork to handle the heavy lifting of your business administration and growth research.
