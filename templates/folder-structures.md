## Setup Tips

1. **Create the `Cowork-Output` folder** вЂ” This is where Claude saves all generated reports and logs
2. **Use date-based subfolders** for financial data (YYYY-MM format)
3. **Grant Cowork access to the top-level folder** вЂ” It can navigate subfolders automatically
4. **Don't share sensitive folders** like passwords, personal medical records, or credentials
5. **Start small** вЂ” Begin with one section (like Finance) and expand as you get comfortable
